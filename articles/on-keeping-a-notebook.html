On Keeping a Notebook

23 June 2026

There is a particular kind of thought that only arrives once the pen is already moving. It does not show up to be transcribed; it shows up because the writing made room for it.

This is placeholder text for a piece still taking shape. The argument, roughly, is that a notebook is less a record than a workshop — and that the friction of writing slowly is a feature, not a bug.
